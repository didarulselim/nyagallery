Why Us

NEW YORK ART GALLERY AND FRAMING has been committed to preserving your artwork, photos, and family keepsakes, while at the same time making them look fabulous! We take great pride in exceeding your expectations. Nothing is too big, too small, or too strange for us to frame!

OUR EXPERIENCE

We have been picture framers, wood-workers, and creative problem-solvers for most of our lives. Owner, Jennifer Burger O’Brien has over 20 years of experience and is a Certified Picture Framer (CPF through the Professional Picture Framers Association). You can rest assured we will approach your framing project armed with the knowledge necessary to ensure it’s longevity. This isn’t just a job to us, this is our profession and our passion.

OUR FRAME SELECTION

We carry one of the largest and most unique selections of frames from around the world, that suit every budget. Hand-painted, welded steel frames created by a surfer in California, sit alongside traditional hand-carved, gilded, and historically accurate frames made in New York. Simple, yet elegant hardwoods, sustainably sourced in Vermont, mingle with contemporary European mouldings – rich in their hand-rubbed patinas and decorative patterns. We take great pride in the thousands of options!

OUR STANDARDS

One might call us obsessive…we prefer meticulous! No detail is too small for our eye. We pride ourselves on getting every aspect of your job perfect…and doing it all on-site. Whether it is fine-tuning the colored matboard on your family photo, or choosing the correct museum mounting for that valuable drawing you acquired from your great aunt – we will expertly guide you to the right option for your piece.

OUR PROMISE

We want you 100% satisfied, which is why we ask questions and listen to everything you share regarding your décor, space, and taste. We then guide you to the right options…we love a good collaboration! We also want you to feel comfortable with the cost, while at the same time love your choice. Our huge selection enables us to work within your budget without compromising design. There truly is a frame for everyone!

Custom Glass Makers

Whether you want to protect your treasured artwork behind glass is one of the most important decisions you will have to make after selecting a frame. No matter what kind of art you have there are certain advantages to displaying it behind glass. They include keeping fingers off the surface of a picture. Modern glass can protect an artwork from the ravages of ultra-violet rays. One of the greatest modern inventions in framing has been the creation of glass with minimal glare.

Design Consultation

This face to face meeting gives us a chance to learn about your vision and desires for the art that you love. Using our expertise, we guide you through the selection process deciding on colors, textures, materials, composition, balance, and space, all with the goal of honoring your artwork. Once a plan is in place, the framing production can begin.

PRODUCTION

With over 120 years of combined framing experience, our team will use the time-tested, artisan techniques that ensure your framed art is made properly and will last forever. Not only will your framing be produced with craftsman care and attention to detail, but we guarantee that you'll love the finished product. And with that, your beautiful, custom work of art is ready to be showcased.

HANGING

Time to display and adore your art. You can employ our team to deliver and hang your framed art for you using the strongest and safest methods in the industry. Or we can provide you with materials and instructions to install it yourself. The framing process may end here, but the delight your artwork instills has just begun.
